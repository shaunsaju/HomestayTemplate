Description

Achayan's Homestay is located in the heart of the historic center of Allapuzha in an extremely characteristic, quite and lively area within short walk distance to all sites and is surrounded by the extraordinary beauty of churches, buildings, shops and monuments.Achayan's Homestay is part of a lovingly restored 1800 Tharavadu.

On entering this charming hotel in Allapuzha, you will immediately sense its special intimate atmosphere that makes you feel like being in your own Kerala Style home. Each detail has been passionately chosen and each room deserves a visit. Achayan's Homestay style mixes valuable antiques and original artworks with an unexpected eclectic contemporary twist. The entire House recalls the ancient ‘Palazzi’ of times past where young European aristocrats lived while discovering the beauty and the artistic mysteries of Italy.

The special charm and the cosy mood of Allapuzha will make you feel as a true Keralite in Kerala.
